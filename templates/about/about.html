Our Story

Lifesource Irrigation is a full-service irrigation, gardening, and exterior lighting company with over three decades of experience serving New York City.

From extensive transformations of rooftop gardens to power washing in hard-to-reach terraces, irrigation installations for hands-off plant care to lighting arrangements that set the balcony aglow, the Lifesource team is dedicated to understanding client needs and delivering exceptional results.

Meet Declan Keane

Founder & Principal

Born in Kilnamona, Co. Clare, Ireland, Declan brings over 30 years of irrigation experience to every project. With an Agricultural Science degree from University College Dublin and a Higher Diploma in Education, he combines scientific knowledge with practical expertise.

Declan arrived in New York City on March 25th, 1988, and quickly established himself as a pioneer in bringing drip irrigation technology to NYC's unique rooftop environments. Previously operating as "Estelle Irrigation," he founded Lifesource to expand his vision of transforming urban outdoor spaces.

His deep understanding of plants, water systems, and NYC's challenging microclimates has made Lifesource the trusted choice for discerning property owners across Manhattan.
